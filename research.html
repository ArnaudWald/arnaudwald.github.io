Research & Scientific Interests

A few words about my research.

I explore the dynamics of neurodegenerative diseases to learn statistical models that characterize average and individual progressions of the disease.


As part of the Aramis Lab, located at the Brain and Spine Institute, at the Pitié Salpétrière Hospital, in Paris, I develop new tools to analyze the progression of neurodegenerative diseases, especially Alzheimer's Disease. I contribute to explore new ways to understand the brain evolution thanks to Machine Learning tools.


The main goal we are trying to reach are multiples
  • Get a better understanding of the disease progression for the doctors
  • Not only to be able to predict individual futur stage but also to better understand one's particular trajectory
  • Give tools to clinical trials in order to measure the impact of a drug versus what should have had happend that we try to determine
