I'm a PhD Student in Machine Learning, under the supervision of Stanley Durrleman and Stéphanie Allassonnière. I belong to the Centre de Mathématiques Appliquées de l'Ecole Polytechnique while working at the Aramis Lab, part of the Brain and Spine Institute, located in the Pitié-Salpétrière Hospital, in the heart of Paris.

Beyond medical application, I love to develop tools or share my knowledge in many applications to help people achieve their goals, as long as they involve motivating & positive impacts. This includes various projects from a chatbot for a Social-and-Solidarity association to a web-app to choose and analyze the different political parties at the French election through teaching at University or a Data Science Summer School.
